Projects

  • HealthSystem:
    The purpose of this project is to design and build a lightweight and easily modifiable health data system. This design will be modular to promote customization, and avoid large monolithic repositories. Intended features are to include a database schema, ETL tools, PACS imaging database, and automation services.
